# TL;DR it looks like ios and needs PWAs to use certain things

I first heard about /e/ OS around 2019, it appealed to the side of me that likes to make things look like things they aren't, because of Bliss Launcher. Bliss was buggy on other ROMs and my phone at the time was unsupported.

Here's how it looks on my device. 



In 2020 I got myself a Pixel 3A, and earlier this year, around the time the alpha of /e/OS 1.0, I installed it on my device. It hasn't left the device since.

Currently, some apps can only be used if they're PWAs, progressive web apps, or if you have magisk installed. That's not a deal breaker for me, but it may be for you, so be prepared for jank.

The lock screen is vanilla android, which is preferable to iOS's for me. It is definitely less unique than Bliss is, but that is perfectly fine.



The most annoying bug in this rom is the weird OAuth issues, at first it was just calendar and contact sync, but that was fixed in an update, and it introduced a bug in the email oauth which just completely died this update.



I can't completely recommend this rom to anyone, if you're okay with jank and don't need Google dependent apps, then 100%, go for it.
