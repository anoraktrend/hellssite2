# TL;DR it looks like ios and needs PWAs to use certain things

I first heard about /e/ OS around 2019, it appealed to the side of me that likes to make things look like things they aren't, because of Bliss Launcher. Bliss was buggy on other ROMs and my phone at the time was unsupported.

In 2020 I got myself a Pixel 3A, and earlier this year, around the time the alpha of /e/OS 1.0, I installed it on my device. It hasn't left the device since.
